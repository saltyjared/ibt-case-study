About Us

Intuitive BioTechnologies (IBT) is a service-based contract research organization, specializing in cell-based in vitro assays and cell characterization services. Additionally, IBT offers protein quantification and biomarker detection services. We at IBT aim to provide clients with the most cost-effective and reliable testing solutions, allowing you to meet your needs and expectations.
